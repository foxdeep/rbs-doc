The **Items** section in the RBS Admin Portal's Resource Management area lets you manage the individual items that make up your resources. This gives you a lot of control over your resource components, helping you keep track of assets and maintain them properly.

To see the items for a specific resource, just click on its row in the Resource Management table.

### Managing Items (CRUD Operations)

This section explains how you can manage items using standard operations: Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD).
