The Customer Management section within the RBS Admin Portal provides administrators with a streamlined way to access and view customer information related to resource bookings. This feature offers a view of essential customer details, including their name, account, email, and phone number.

---

Administrators can conveniently access and view personal information of customers who have made resource bookings on the site. This includes details such as the customer's name, account information, email address, and phone number. This data is valuable for communication and contact purposes, allowing administrators to effectively engage with customers who have interacted with the resource booking system.

The ability to access and view customer information is essential for maintaining clear and efficient communication with users and customers who utilize the resource booking system. It ensures that administrators have the necessary contact details at their disposal for any communication or support needs.
