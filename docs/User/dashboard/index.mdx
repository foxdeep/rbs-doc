Welcome to the User's Dashboard, your centralized control center for efficient and user-friendly resource management. This dashboard is designed to provide you with a seamless and organized experience, ensuring you have all the tools and information you need at your fingertips.

- ✨ Calendar View for Booking Events

  The `My Reservations` section provides a calendar view, allowing you to easily visualize and manage your upcoming and past booking events. This feature simplifies scheduling and helps you stay organized.

- ✨ Streamlined Booking Management

  The User's Dashboard empowers you to effortlessly manage your resource bookings. Whether you're tracking your past reservations, checking the status of your current bookings, the `My Orders` section offers a comprehensive view of your booking history. It ensures you remain organized and informed about your resource allocation.

- ✨ Site Interaction Simplified

  The `My Sites` section on the dashboard is your gateway to managing your interactions with various sites. It allows you to navigate and engage with the sites you're associated with, making it easy to access the resources and information that matter most to you.

- ✨ User-Centric Design

  Our User's Dashboard is designed with your convenience in mind. It offers a user-centric approach to resource management, ensuring you have easy access to your booking history and site interactions.
